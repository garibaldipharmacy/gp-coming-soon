Why Us

We are local pharmacists stepping up to address the needs of Squamish. Different from the big-box and corporate chain pharmacies that dominate Squamish, we are starting up with a vision of providing a better pharmacy experience along with our expertise in creating customized medicine. Our desire is to also make primary medical care more accessible to Squamish residents. Our in-house medical clinic and partnership with Terra Nova Medical Clinics will make this a reality.
