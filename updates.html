New Update October 27, 2023 (9:00 PM)

Terra Nova Medical Clinic (Squamish) will have a physician starting family practice in February 2024. The clinic is working on creating a patient intake portal for Squamish residents where patients may register to be put on a list. We will have a link to the portal on this website once it is completed.

Garibaldi Pharmacy is still expected to be up and running by early December and will serve patients wanting their medications, immunizations, and those requiring treatment for minor ailments and contraception under BC's expanded scope of practice for pharmacists.

October 3, 2023 (11:00 AM)

There are some delays in construction. We are expecting to have the pharmacy up and running by early December and the clinic to start up the first week of January of the new year.

Thank you for your patience. We are eager to serve our community soon.
